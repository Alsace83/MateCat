How Matecat Helps Objective Quality Mesuraments

MateCat uses an analitical approach to quality. It defines the maximum number of minor issues (major counts as 2 minor) and use the data provided by the reviser to calculate an objective score.

Based on your needs, you can agree with your provider the quality you expect. For some people/projects "Good" or "Acceptable" may be ok, others will require an "Excellent" score from your providers.

Score Calculation

Matecat uses the following formula to calculate the num of tollerated issues for the project:

(Num of Payable Words / 2500) * Num of Minor Error Tollerated

For each type of issue, Matecat calculates the score using in this way:

Num of Issues Found / Num of Tollerated Issues

The final vote is calculated as the sum of the single category votes.
